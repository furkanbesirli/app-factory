Child Safety Standards

Application: Camzey
Package ID: com.cmzymobile.aab

Zero Tolerance Policy: Camzey has a zero-tolerance policy for any content involving the exploitation of children. We strictly prohibit Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any behavior that puts minors at risk.

1. Age Requirement

Users must be at least 18 years old to use Camzey. We use various methods to verify age and detect underage users. Anyone found to be a minor will be permanently banned from the platform.

2. Prohibited Behavior

Prohibited actions include but are not limited to:

  • Sharing or requesting sexual content involving minors.
  • Grooming or predatory behavior.
  • Endangering the safety or well-being of children.

3. Reporting and Enforcement

We provide easy-to-use reporting tools in every chat session. All reports related to child safety are prioritized for immediate review. We cooperate fully with law enforcement and report illegal activities to the relevant authorities, including NCMEC.

4. Automated Moderation

Camzey employs advanced AI and automated filtering to detect and block prohibited content in real-time. Our moderation team monitors the platform 24/7 to ensure a safe environment.

5. Contact for Safety Issues

If you encounter content that violates these standards, please report it immediately using the in-app tools or contact our safety team:

Email: safety@cmzymobile.com
